Background

To keep documents flowing for reconciliation, clients are notified whenever transactions are missing supporting documents.

* A **real-time notification** is triggered when a transaction is flagged as Missing Attachment, while
* Monthly reminders highlight any outstanding transactions that still require documentation. Notifications direct clients to a dedicated document management dashboard, providing a clear overview of missing attachments, recently uploaded documents, and outstanding actions.

From there, clients can review affected transactions and upload supporting documents individually or in bulk.

## Goal

Notify the client (in-app + push) in real time when a transaction is Missing Attachment and remind them monthly while documents are still outstanding, routing them to the Missing Attachment list to bulk-upload.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. **Real-time notification when a transaction is flagged Missing Attachment**

GIVEN a transaction is flagged Missing Attachment
WHEN the flag is set
THEN the client receives a real-time notification via in-app and push (see Channels table)

2. **Monthly reminder for still-missing documents**

GIVEN transactions remain Missing Attachment
WHEN the monthly reminder runs
THEN the client receives a monthly reminder (in-app + push) summarising the outstanding transactions

3. **Notification redirects to the Missing Attachment list**

GIVEN the client opens a missing-document notification or reminder
WHEN they action it
THEN they are taken to the dedicated Missing Attachment list (Client Story C1)

4. **Bulk upload from the list**

GIVEN the client is on the Missing Attachment list from a notification
WHEN they provide the required files
THEN they can bulk-upload them (Client Story C3)

5. **Reminders stop when resolved**

GIVEN a transaction becomes Attachment Added (matched)
WHEN reconciliation is satisfied for it
THEN it is excluded from further notifications and monthly reminders

## Rules

**Notification Types**

| Type | Trigger | Cadence |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Ongoing (real-time) | Transaction flagged Missing Attachment | Immediately on flag |
| Periodic reminder | Transactions still Missing Attachment | Monthly **(To be Confirmed with Accountant)** |

**Channels**

| Channel | Used |
| --- | --- |
| In-app | Yes |
| Push | Yes |
| Email / WhatsApp | No (notifications are in-app + push) |

## Notes

* Client platform only.
* Reminders are monthly and continue until the transaction is Attachment Added.
* Grouping: the real-time notification is a grouped count ("X transactions need documents") to avoid noise
* Monthly reminder timing (To confirm which day of the month).
