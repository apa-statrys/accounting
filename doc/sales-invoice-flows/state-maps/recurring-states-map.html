Recurring · state map

Every state of the invoice and the series

Scoped to a recurring invoice only — one series and each occurrence it generates. Two objects, two scopes: the invoice only ever shows instance actions (send / edit this one / delete); the series owns the template edit + lifecycle. The one cross-dependency: when the series is Paused, the scheduled invoice is "on hold", so Send now drops from primary to a menu override.

The one cross-object rule: series Paused → the scheduled invoice is on hold, so Send now moves from the dock's primary to a menu override, and its subline/badge context reads "Paused". Everything else on the invoice is scoped to that invoice alone.

Once sent, an invoice is independent — Awaiting/Paid invoices keep their own actions and don't change when the series is paused, completed, or cancelled. Pausing/cancelling only affects invoices not yet generated.

Series lifecycle: Active ⇄ Paused (reversible) · → Completed (end reached) · → Cancelled (manual, irreversible). Completed & Cancelled are view-only — no Edit/Pause; Cancel lives in the ⋯ only while Active/Paused.
