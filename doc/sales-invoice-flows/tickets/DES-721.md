# DES-721 — Story 8 (CLIENT) — View Credit Note Details

**Status:** In Progress · **Fetched:** 2026-07-13. Read-only CN preview reached from an invoice's
"Related documents" (and, secondarily, the Credit Notes List / DES-818). Out of scope: creating (719),
refunding (720), editing.

## Acceptance Criteria
1. **Related credit note on the invoice** — invoice shows its linked CN under "Related documents" with
   CN number, type, status; section hidden if none; **at most one CN per invoice**.
2. **Read-only preview** — selecting it opens a non-editable preview with all Displayed Details; no field editable.
3. **View linked invoice** — tapping the related-invoice reference opens that invoice's view screen.
4. **Send** — "Send Credit Note" opens the send flow (email / link / download), reusing the invoice send
   flow; optional; does not affect CN status.

## Displayed Details (read-only)
CN number · **Status ("Applied to [invoice number]" / Cancelled)** · CN date · Reason (dropdown + optional
free text) · Description · Customer name · **Line items (item name, description, quantity, unit, unit price,
amount — one row each)** · CN total · Related invoice (tappable link).

## Notes
- Preview is non-editable; data reflects the linked invoice.
- Send reuses Story 5; no separate download beyond the send flow.
- Multi-invoice CN display = Priority 2, out of scope.
- Open (TBD): can the user cancel from the View CN screen?

### Decision 2026-07-13 (PO) — DES-721 needs NO build changes
The three gaps below were reviewed and all resolved as won't-change; the current CN detail satisfies 721.

1. **Status label "Applied to [invoice number]"** — NOT needed. We keep the short **"Applied"** chip.
2. **Line-item detail** — BUILT as "option C" (adaptive), 2026-07-13. Each credited line shows
   **name + "Credit: qty × unit price"** when it's a clean quantity credit, or **"Credit: Price Adjustment"**
   when it's a value reduction (cancellation only; refund items show no sub-line) + the amount. Applies
   to all CN detail surfaces (invoice-detail Credits, Credit Notes List, invoice-list badge). The register
   seed carries qty/unitPrice per line; the form derives them (unchanged unit price + fewer units = a
   quantity credit). **Per-line `unit` (Hr/day) and per-line `description` are NOT shown** — the PO
   confirmed the qty × price is enough (2026-07-13); full per-line detail (option E) was set aside.
3. **"Related documents" naming / CN type tag** — NOT needed. Section stays "Credits (n)".

**Non-issues:** Cancel-from-view is a ticket TBD (we wire it, harmless); multi-invoice display out of scope.
