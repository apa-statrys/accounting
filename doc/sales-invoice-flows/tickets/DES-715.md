# [DES-715] Sales invoice flows — Story 3 (CLIENT facing side) — Create Sale Invoice

> Fetched live from Jira 2026-07-02. Status: In Progress · Assignee: ayepapa myo · Reporter: Beatrice Ozanne
> https://statrys.atlassian.net/browse/DES-715

## Background

In Block 1 (Sales Invoice) of the Statrys accounting feature, clients need to raise sales invoices
directly in the mobile app, send them to their customers, and have them resolve to Paid once the
money arrives — connecting the accounting side to the payment side.

**Comments — For Recurring Sales Invoice Generation option — see Story 48.**

Clients who bill the same customers on a regular basis — retainers, subscriptions, monthly
services — need a way to generate invoices automatically on a fixed schedule without recreating
them each time.

## Goal

Let a client create, issue, send, and resolve a sales invoice through its full status lifecycle
(Draft → Awaiting Payment → Overdue / Partially Paid / Paid / Void) from the mobile app.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. **Create and fill a draft invoice**
   GIVEN a client is on the invoice list
   WHEN they select "Create Invoice"
   THEN the system creates an invoice in **Draft** status (editable) and displays the create-invoice form
   AND the client can select a client/customer, line items, currency, and a **due date**
   AND a Draft invoice can be deleted (removed from system); deletion is only available in Draft

2. **Issue the invoice**
   GIVEN a valid draft invoice
   WHEN the client selects "Create" / issue
   THEN the system **generates the invoice number**, validates required fields, and moves the invoice to **Awaiting Payment**
   AND the invoice number cannot be changed after issuing

3. **Send the invoice from the app**
   GIVEN an issued (Awaiting Payment) invoice
   WHEN the client chooses to send it
   THEN the system emails it as a PDF to the client's stored Email Address **and/or** generates a shareable link
   AND if the client has no Email Address on file, the system prompts to add one while the shareable link remains available

4. **Edit an issued invoice**
   GIVEN an invoice in Awaiting Payment or Overdue status
   WHEN the client makes a permitted edit
   THEN the system applies the limited edit, regenerates the invoice document, and re-sends / notifies the customer

5. **Cancel an issued invoice with a Credit Note**
   GIVEN an invoice in Awaiting Payment or Overdue status
   WHEN the client selects "Cancel with Credit Note" and completes the Credit Note flow
   THEN the invoice moves to **Void** (non-editable)

6. **Reconcile against a payment**
   GIVEN an issued invoice and incoming transactions on the payment side
   WHEN the system auto-suggests a matching transaction and the client confirms the match
   THEN the system sets the status based on the matched amount (see Reconciliation Outcomes)
   AND a fully matched invoice becomes **Paid** (non-editable)

## Rules

**Invoice Status Matrix**

| Status | Editable? | Entered When | Deletable? | Terminal? |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Draft | Yes | On create, before issuing | Yes | No |
| Awaiting Payment | Limited | Invoice issued | No | No |
| Overdue | Limited | Awaiting Payment and due date has passed, unpaid (client is notified) | No | No |
| Partially Paid | No | Reconciliation matches a payment less than the total | No | No |
| Paid | No | Reconciliation matches the full total (or overpayment) | No | Yes |
| Void | No | Cancel with Credit Note completed | No | Yes |

**Reconciliation Outcomes**

| Matched Amount vs Invoice Total | Resulting Status |
| --- | --- |
| Equal to total | Paid |
| Less than total | Partially Paid (remainder still due) |
| Greater than total | Paid, with the overpayment flagged for review (credit/refund) |

**Send Channels**

| Channel | Detail |
| --- | --- |
| Email | PDF sent to the client's stored Email Address (prompt to add one if missing) |
| Shareable link | Link the client can share with the customer; always available |

## Notes

- Surface: created and sent from the **mobile app**; the Operation Manager **back office is web**.
- **Overdue** notifies the Statrys client (the sender) only — they decide whether to chase the
  customer. "Overdue" itself is a time-based transition, not a user action.
- **Editing an issued invoice** is limited and regenerates + re-sends the document; this differs
  from the Edit Client rule, which only propagates client-detail changes to draft invoices.
- **Reconciliation** here is covered at the status-outcome level; the detailed
  transaction-matching mechanism is a separate story.
- Out of scope: the Credit Note flow itself (referenced) and the overpayment resolution mechanics
  (credit note vs refund), which are flagged for the client / back office.
