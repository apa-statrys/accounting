# [DES-719] Sales invoice flows — Story 6 (CLIENT facing side) — Create (and Edit) Sales Credit Notes

> Fetched live from Jira 2026-07-10 (ticket last updated 2026-07-08; "Story revised entirely"
> 2026-07-07 by Anne per Kiru's feedback). Status: In Progress · Reporter: Beatrice Ozanne
> https://statrys.atlassian.net/browse/DES-719

## Background

Clients sometimes need to cancel or reduce an unpaid sales invoice — a customer cancels an order, a
price is corrected, or a discount is applied. A credit note is a formal document that reduces what
the customer owes. It can be raised against any unpaid invoice for the same customer, and a single
credit note can be linked to **multiple** unpaid invoices at once. Once applied, a credit note cannot
be edited — if raised in error, the client cancels it entirely (full reversal) and starts a new one.

## Goal

Let a client create a sales credit note against one or more unpaid invoices (Awaiting Payment /
Overdue) for the same customer, apply it to reduce or cancel the outstanding balance, and cancel it
entirely if it was raised in error.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. **Client opens the New Credit Note form** — opens in Draft; pre-fills the Pre-filled Fields;
   client fills the Client-Filled Fields before confirming.
2. **Client selects and allocates credit across invoices** — select one or more unpaid invoices for
   the same customer; each shows its outstanding balance; client enters a credit amount per invoice;
   each allocation ≤ that invoice's outstanding; the sum of allocations must equal the total CN
   amount before proceeding.
3. **Client confirms and creates the credit note** — "Create Credit Note" validates all required
   fields; on pass the CN is created with status **"Applied to [invoice number(s)]"**; CN number is
   system-generated (read-only); each linked invoice updates per the Credit Outcome table; **the CN
   is not editable after this point**.
4. **Client cancels an applied credit note** — "Cancel Credit Note" + confirm → status **Cancelled**;
   all effects on every linked invoice fully reversed; reversing GL entry posted; CN number retired
   (cannot be reused); cancelled CN cannot be reinstated (open a New Credit Note to start again).
5. **Client sends the credit note** — email / shareable link / download, reusing the invoice send
   flow; email includes the CN document, linked invoice number(s), remaining balance per invoice, and
   the due date; sending is optional (CN is applied regardless of send).
6. **Create failure is recoverable** — clear error, retains all entered details + allocations, retry
   without re-entering data.
7. **Client deletes a draft Credit Note** — Draft only; confirmation prompt; permanently removes the
   CN; deletion only available in Draft.

## Rules

### New Credit Note vs Create Credit Note
- **New Credit Note** (invoice list / invoice detail) → opens the blank CN form; nothing created yet.
- **Create Credit Note** (button at the bottom of the form) → submits, creates, and immediately
  applies the CN to the linked invoice(s).

### Pre-filled Fields (editable before Create, except CN number)
| Field | Pre-filled from | Editable before Create? |
| --- | --- | --- |
| CN number | System — CN-YYYY-###### (from 000001) | No — read-only always |
| Customer name | Selected customer record | Yes |
| Associated invoice(s) | Invoice(s) selected by client | Yes — add/remove |
| CN date | Today (default) | Yes — DD/MM/YYYY |
| Due date | Earliest due date among linked invoice(s) | Yes — DD/MM/YYYY |
| CN currency | Currency of the linked invoice(s) | No — fixed to invoice currency |
| Payment method | Statrys Primary VA (default) | Yes |

### Client-Filled Fields (all required)
Reason (enum: Return / Defect / Pricing error / Goodwill / Dispute — plus optional free text),
Description (string), Quantity (2 dp, max 999,999.99), Unit (unit/hour/day/month/session/pair),
Unit Price (2 dp, CN currency), Amount (Qty × Unit Price, auto/read-only), Allocation per invoice
(2 dp each; must sum to CN total).

### Multi-Invoice Allocation Rules
- Only unpaid invoices (Awaiting / Overdue) for the **same customer and same currency** are shown;
  other-currency invoices are excluded and cannot be selected.
- Each allocation must not exceed that invoice's outstanding balance.
- Sum of allocations must equal the CN total; "Create Credit Note" disabled until satisfied.

### Credit Outcome
- Credited = full invoice amount → invoice marked **Void** ("keep consistent terminology" = Cancelled).
- Credited < full → invoice stays **Awaiting Payment**; outstanding reduced by the credited amount.

### Carried-Over Data (pre-fill from invoice)
Client info (editable), linked invoice number (stored as link, shown on CN), line items/products
(editable for partial credit), currency (same as invoice, not editable).

### Credit Note Status Matrix (revised — 3 states)
| Status | Meaning | Editable? | Cancellable? |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Draft | Form open, not yet confirmed/applied | Yes — all fields except CN number | No — client can delete (record removed, number retired) |
| Applied to [invoice number(s)] | CN confirmed & applied | **No** | Yes — full reversal |
| Cancelled | CN cancelled, all effects reversed | No | No |

### Credit Note Validation Rules
Required fields complete → can create; required editable fields empty → not created (inform user);
CN issue date invalid (must be DD/MM/YYYY) → not created; credited amount exceeds invoice
outstanding → not created (inform user).

## Notes
- CN number sequence CN-YYYY-###### is independent of invoice numbering; assigned **when the form
  opens** (before completion/confirmation).
- CN is applied immediately on "Create Credit Note" — no separate apply step (Draft → Applied).
- Cancelling a CN is always a full reversal of all linked allocations simultaneously; partial
  cancellation (one invoice's allocation only) is not supported.
- Journal entries: issuing a CN reverses related journal entries (specified in the Chart of
  Accounts / Journal story, not here).
- **Out of scope:** the refund flow (DES-720), the View Credit Note flow (DES-721), and journal posting.
- Reason field required: dropdown (return / defect / pricing error / goodwill / dispute) + optional free text.
- **Go-live flag: this feature must be turned OFF on Prod at launch.**
- Surface: client side = mobile app; Operation Manager back office = web.

## Things Not Clear (open)

- Validate the delete-draft-CN behaviour with engineers.

### DECISION 2026-07-10 (PO confirmed) — single invoice only, for now
We ship **one credit note ↔ one invoice** (the Qonto model, which our current build already
implements). **Multi-invoice allocation is deferred** — not built for this pass. The revised ticket's
multi-invoice requirement (AC2, Multi-Invoice Allocation Rules) is out of scope until a later phase.
Competitor landscape that informed this: Qonto = 1:1 (matches us); Xero/QuickBooks/Zoho = multi with a
"remaining credit / Open→Closed" model. So going single-invoice keeps us aligned with our Qonto
reference and avoids the forced-full-allocation UX the multi model would need.

**Still different (build → ticket), pending later decisions:**

   decision above** (ticket's biggest new requirement; revisit in a later phase).
