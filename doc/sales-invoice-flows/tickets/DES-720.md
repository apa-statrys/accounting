# DES-720 — Story 7 (CLIENT) — Refund Client with Credit Notes

**Status:** In Progress · **Fetched:** 2026-07-14 ("Final story updated" 2026-07-08; KR post-meeting
comments 2026-07-03). Refund a **Paid** invoice via a credit note. Golive: CN feature off on prod.

## Acceptance Criteria (final body)
1. From a Paid invoice → "Refund with Credit Note" → New CN form pre-filled; **line items NOT pre-filled**
   (client fills Description/Qty/Unit/Unit Price/Amount); **link one or more** paid invoices (same
   customer + currency); one refund CN per paid invoice.
2. Create CN → each linked invoice **Paid → Pending Refund**; refund ≤ amount paid; not editable after.
3. Choose refund method — **Bank transfer** or **Mark as Refunded**.
4. **Bank transfer** → pre-filled draft handed to the BA payment flow; client confirms (no auto-exec);
   executed transfer auto-reconciles; accountant validates + posts GL; invoice → Refunded; in-app notify.
5. **External / Mark as Refunded** → client records proof; invoice **stays Pending Refund** with
   "Refund submitted — awaiting accountant confirmation"; accountant posts GL → Refunded.
6. Refund outcome: full → **Refunded**; partial → **Refunded** with outstanding refund shown ("Partial Flag").

## External Refund Fields (Mark as Refunded)
Date of refund (req) · **Bank account used — dropdown of Statrys + registered external BAs** (req) ·
Refund amount (req) · Proof: **file upload OR reference number — at least one required**.
